Our Services

📊 Statistics Services

Regression Analysis

Analyzes the relationship between dependent and independent variables. Helps predict future outcomes such as sales, demand, or cost trends using historical data.

Hypothesis Testing

Tests assumptions using statistical methods to determine whether business decisions or changes produce significant results.

Correlation Analysis

Measures the strength and direction of the relationship between variables. Helps understand data behavior before deeper analysis.

📈 Economics Services

Demand Estimation

Estimates how quantity demanded responds to price changes. Helps firms make effective pricing and production decisions.

Profit Maximization

Identifies the output level where profit is highest by analyzing revenue and cost behavior, supported with graphical visualization.

Cost Behaviour Analysis

Studies fixed, variable, and total costs to understand how costs change with output, supporting planning and cost control decisions.
