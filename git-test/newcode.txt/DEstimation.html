📊 Demand Estimation – Decision Assistant

📌 What is Demand Estimation?
Demand Estimation helps businesses understand how customer demand changes when price changes. This tool converts raw price–quantity data into clear business decisions.
  • Should we reduce price to increase sales?
  • Will a price increase improve revenue?
  • Is demand sensitive or stable?
📐 Models Used in This Tool:
1. Linear Demand Function
Q = a + bP

Where:
a = Base demand (intercept)
b = Price sensitivity (slope)
2. Price Elasticity of Demand
E = (ΔQ / ΔP) × (P / Q)
