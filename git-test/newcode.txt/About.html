Project Overview

The Business Analytics & Decision Support System is an innovative web-based platform designed to help users analyze business data and convert it into meaningful decisions.

Problem We Address

  • Difficulty interpreting raw numerical data
  • Manual calculations are slow and error-prone
  • Lack of visual decision support
  • No direct managerial recommendations

Our Solution

We automate calculations, visualize insights, and provide decision-oriented conclusions for better business outcomes.
